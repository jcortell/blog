Acquiring a work of art from the Guggenheim Museum in New York

2 September 2014 in Art, General, Life's pleasures, NY, Personal, Society and politics
August 30th my wife and I acquired another work of art for our collection. In this case we chose Colombian contemporary artist Carlos Motta: “Brief history of US Interventions in Latin America since 1946, 2005/14” (Guggenheim UBS MAP Purchase Fund 2014.39). It is part of a series produced by Carlos Motta between 2005 and 2009 that present two chronologies of events in Latin America: one of U.S. interventions in the region since 1946, and one of the areas leftist guerrilla movements.
