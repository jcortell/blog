Testing a simple hack

4 January 2014 in Geek Fun, General, Hacking, Personal, Technology
I use qtranslate as a multi-language plugin for my blog. It`s an excellent solution, but the problem is that everytime Word Press is updated, it takes days, and sometimes weeeks, for the plugin author to update it. So, tired of waiting, I have found a quick and simple hack to make it work with the latests WP 3.8 update: edit qtranslate.php to define(‘QT_SUPPORTED_WP_VERSION, ‘3.8); You`re welcome 😉
