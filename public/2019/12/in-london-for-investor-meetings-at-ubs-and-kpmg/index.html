In London for investor meetings at UBS and KPMG

5 December 2019

December 4th and 5th I traveled from Helsinki to London for two investor related meetings, at KPMG and UBS.

Wednesday December 4th I was invited to the China Investors Dinner at KPMG’s “Number 20” Office. Thursday December 5th I went to UBS 5 Broadgate to meet their Healthcare team to discuss investment options.

Festive lights everywhere, amazing art galleries, and startling income inequality concentrated in a few blocks of the city center.

As I’ve done before, I stayed at the Bailey’s Hotel. A conveniently located classic.
